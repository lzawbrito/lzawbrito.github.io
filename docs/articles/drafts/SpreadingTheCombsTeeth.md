# Spreading the Comb's Teeth
## Lie Algebras and Deleuze's _Difference and Repetition_
`08/24/2022`

Gilles Deleuze's early work _Difference and Repetition_, understood to be his first exercise in breaking philosophical ground as well as serving as a tone-setter for his better-known _Capitalism and Schizophrenia_, provides an dense study of its two titular concepts, exploring the possibility of their ontological primacy in service of studying experience in a metaphysical light. The novelty in his approach lies in privileging "difference in itself" as ontologically prior to the relata in which it signifies a difference.
