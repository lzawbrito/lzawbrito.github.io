"The Sky Drapes, a Cloth provides a subtle account of power, and our emotional and physical reliance on other people. Both touching and caustic, this EP constructs soul as body, power as physical structure, and the personality as a building. This EP also features Brito’s most complex and touching guitar playing, making great use of the sound of classical guitar, along with well-constructed harmony that provides great emotional weight to his words and to the melodies he sings."

-Ted Howard
